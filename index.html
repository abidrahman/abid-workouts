Primary objectives

What we are training for

  • Aug 1: guided Mt. Baker summit readiness.
  • Target: sustain ~1,000 vertical ft/hour with a pack.
  • July 25/26: 1,600 yd supported Lake Union swim.
  • Ongoing: 3 short strength sessions/week led by RDLs, weighted pull-ups, and Bulgarian split squats.
  • Constraint: running paused; avoid shoulder-aggravating push work.

Month 1 intent

Build rhythm, then capacity

June starts with rhythm and baseline work, then builds swim endurance, mountain-specific durability, and strength consistency while keeping shin and shoulder pain at or below 2/10.

Macro plan

Training phase timeline

The block progresses from consistency, to capacity, to specificity, then tapers into the Lake Union swim and Mt. Baker summit.

Gear timeline

Near-term purchases / tests

  • Confirm Mt. Baker guide gear list and estimated pack weights.
  • Get boots early enough to break them in before July.
  • Start wetsuit research in June; acquire by late June / early July.
  • Plan open-water support logistics before July exposures.
